About Organizr

Effortless Task Management for Everyone

📝 Welcome to Organizr

Organizr is a simple yet powerful task management system designed to help users efficiently organize, track, and manage their daily tasks. Built as part of our academic journey, this project helped sharpen our skills in coding, collaboration, and real-world software development.

🎯 Key Features

  • 📌 Add new tasks easily
  • ✅ Mark tasks as completed
  • 🗑 Remove tasks you no longer need
  • 📆 Monitor your daily productivity
