🔹 Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • Q1: How do I create a new task?
    Go to the dashboard, click “Add Task,” fill in the details, and hit “Save.”
  • Q2: How can I edit or delete a task?
    Open a task and choose “Edit” or “Delete.”
  • Q3: Can I set deadlines or reminders?
    Yes, you can set both when adding or editing a task.
  • Q4: How do I mark a task as complete?
    Click the checkbox next to the task or press the “Complete” button.
